### Practical read for teams shipping quickly

1. Teams with `10+ commits / 48h` are paying a coordination tax now. They are the best near-term design-partner and pilot targets because release drift risk is immediate.
2. Single-maintainer velocity (high commits, low author diversity) implies fragility under growth. Pluxx should be positioned as process hardening, not just packaging convenience.
3. Mixed commit types (`feat` + `fix` + `chore` + `revert`) indicate active experimentation. These teams benefit most from deterministic outputs and compatibility verification gates.
4. Low/zero-commit repos are still prospects, but generally not urgent-buying prospects this week. They fit nurture or “next release prep” messaging.
