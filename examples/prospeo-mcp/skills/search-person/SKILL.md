# Search People

Use the `prospeo_search_person` tool to find prospects matching specific criteria.

## When to Use

- "Find CROs at Series B SaaS companies"
- "Who are the engineering leaders at companies using Kubernetes?"
- "Build a list of VPs of Marketing at fintech startups in NYC"

## Available Filters

All filters are optional. Combine them to narrow your search:

- `person_job_title` — titles to include/exclude
- `person_seniority` — C-Suite, Vice President, Director, Manager, etc.
- `person_department` — Sales, Marketing, Engineering, etc.
- `person_location` — country or city
- `company_industry` — Software Development, Financial Services, etc.
- `company_headcount_range` — "1-10", "11-50", "51-200", "201-500", etc.
- `company_technology` — Salesforce, React, AWS, etc.
- `company_funding` — Seed, Series A, Series B, etc.
- `max_person_per_company` — limit contacts per company (default: no limit)

## Cost

1 credit per page (25 results per page).
