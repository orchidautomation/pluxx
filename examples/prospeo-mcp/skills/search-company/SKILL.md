# Search Companies

Use the `prospeo_search_company` tool to find companies matching specific criteria.

## When to Use

- "Find Series A fintech companies with 50-200 employees"
- "What companies in healthcare are using Snowflake?"
- "Build a list of AI startups founded after 2022 in San Francisco"

## Available Filters

All filters are optional:

- `company_industry` — industry to include/exclude
- `company_headcount_range` — "1-10", "11-50", "51-200", etc.
- `company_location` — country or city
- `company_funding` — Seed, Series A, Series B, etc.
- `company_technology` — specific technologies to filter by
- `company_founded` — min/max founding year

## Cost

1 credit per page (25 results per page).
