## Prospeo Plugin

Find verified work emails, enrich contacts and companies from 200M+ records.

### Available Tools

- `prospeo_enrich_person` — Get verified email, title, LinkedIn, mobile, and job history for a person
- `prospeo_search_person` — Search contacts by title, seniority, industry, tech, funding stage
- `prospeo_enrich_company` — Get industry, funding, tech stack, headcount, and revenue for a company
- `prospeo_search_company` — Search companies by industry, size, funding, technology
- `prospeo_bulk_enrich_person` — Enrich up to 50 people in one call
- `prospeo_bulk_enrich_company` — Enrich up to 50 companies in one call
- `prospeo_account_info` — Check remaining credits, plan, and renewal date

### Credit Costs

- Enrich person/company: 1 credit
- Search (per page, 25 results): 1 credit
- Mobile number: 10 credits

### Environment

`PROSPEO_API_KEY` must be set. Get your key at https://app.prospeo.io/api
