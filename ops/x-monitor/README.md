# X Monitor

Launch-focused X monitor for Pluxx.

The goal is not generic MCP chatter. The goal is to find builder and company posts where Pluxx has a realistic path to help with cross-host packaging, rollout, maintenance, and distribution.

## Priorities

- official company launch posts for MCP servers
- founder or builder launch posts for new MCP servers
- official company launch posts for Claude Code plugins
- one-host launches that are likely to hit cross-host packaging drift next
- explicit distribution, install, auth, or rollout pain from the team shipping the thing

## Avoid

- generic commentary about MCP
- reposts, quote-post reactions, and trend summaries
- security discourse unless it comes directly from a team blocked while launching
- broad “MCP is the future” posts with no product launch
- third-party newsletter or aggregator posts unless they link to a primary launch we can use instead
