# Company Research Patterns

Focus queries on what makes a company or market legible:

- product category
- buyer or user
- deployment model
- stage or funding evidence
- notable competitors

Examples:

- `category:company startup building AI sales workflow software for enterprise`
- `category:company developer tools company focused on observability and tracing`
- `category:news company launch developer platform pricing docs`

For competitor sets:

- search the target directly
- search adjacent category terms
- search skeptical or comparative angles, not just self-descriptions
